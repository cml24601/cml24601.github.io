Best In Show

Summary

Christopher Guest does it again with his mockumentary of the world of show dogs. Bizarre characters tell their tails of competing in the most prestigious dog show in the world. Who will win? Who will go home with their tail between their legs? Watch and find out!

Cast

  • Parker Posey as Crazy Bumble Bee Lady
  • Eugene Levy as Two Left Feet
  • Christopher Guest as Nut Guy
  • John Michael Higgins as Serious Announcer
  • Michael McKean as Fabulous Owner
